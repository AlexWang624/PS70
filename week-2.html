Assignment 1: Laser Cut Box

I created a box with lid that has a circle on three chosen sides respectively as decorations:

The dimension of the box is 180 × 120 × 120 mm. Here is the design in Fusion 360:

I was very lucky with making this box because all edges fitted very well with just two cuttings. I made the first cutting in the lab session last week, but the result was not ideal because the laser intensity for scoring was too weak to fold the four vertical walls. I adjusted this problem by increasing the intensity for scoring from 5% to 10% in my second cutting on Wednesday night, and it worked reall well in one go.
