Week 1: Final Project Proposal

02/01/2024

I have two ideas for my final project:

1. To continue my undergrad thesis project Cocoon, an anthropomorphic interactive kinetic sculpture that expresses personal sentiment through a metaphorical shape that imitates the cocoon of a domestic silk moth.

There are some limitations with the current iteration: 1) The mechanism of its motion is very simple. The rotation of the centerpiece is driven by a NEMA 17 Stepper Motor, and I hope to add more complicated motions for more anthropomorphic visual effects; 2) The design of the sculpture could be more customized. In the current version, all materials were purchased from OpenBuilds, and I did not have much freedom in choosing the size of the sculpture. I want to create smaller kinetic sculptures using metals following the theme of my thesis.
